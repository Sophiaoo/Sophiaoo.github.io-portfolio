About

Sophia Ordia-Onwuka (born August 19, 1988) is a Swiss-Nigerian daughter, wife, mother of 2, psychologist, entrepreneur, and web developer. Always being around puzzles as a child, Sophia began understanding patterns and problem solving from an early age. She graduated university in 2012, served in the youth corp and started her first job in 2013. By 2015, her first business was established, House Arodo.

By 2019, she got into networking marketing, starting with Longrich International/Nigeria. And by 2021, she started a course in web development.
